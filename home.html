GLOBAL LEX to host Eurasian New Year Celebration
Nov 30, 2016 4:14 p.m.

GLOBAL LEX, a cultural center for local residents and Lexington’s growing foreign population, is hosting a Eurasian New Year Celebration this Friday, Dec. 2, from 5:30 – 7:30 p.m. This family-friendly event is free to the public and will be the first of its kind in Lexington.

Volunteers revitalize Red Mile Trace Trail
Nov 30, 2016 3:10 p.m.

With the help of 68 volunteers, 18 city employees, and a $20,000 grant from Lowe’s, the Red Mile Place Trail, located between Red Mile Road and Cisco Road, has been revitalized.

Lexington Police partners with Nextdoor, the private social network for neighborhoods
Nov 30, 2016 1:09 p.m.

The Lexington Police Department today announced a partnership with Nextdoor (nextdoor.com), the private social network for neighborhoods, to improve citywide and neighbor-to-neighbor communications.
